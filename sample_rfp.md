# Sample RFP: Cloud CRM Migration

**From:** Sarah Chen, VP of Sales Operations, NovaTech Industries
**To:** [Your Company]
**Date:** June 2026
**Subject:** Request for Proposal - Enterprise CRM Platform Migration

---

## 1. Company Background

NovaTech Industries is a mid-market manufacturing company with 1,200 employees
across 3 offices (Chicago, Austin, Berlin). Our sales team of 180 reps currently
uses a legacy on-premise CRM (Siebel) that is end-of-life in Q1 2027.

## 2. Requirements

### Functional Requirements
- Migrate 5 years of customer data (~2.4M records) from Siebel to a modern
  cloud CRM
- Support for our existing sales process: Lead - Opportunity - Quote - Order
- Mobile app for field sales reps (iOS and Android)
- Real-time dashboards for sales managers with pipeline and forecast views
- Email integration with Microsoft 365

### Technical Requirements
- Cloud-hosted (prefer AWS or Azure, we already have an Azure tenant)
- SSO integration with our Azure Active Directory (Entra ID)
- REST API for integration with our ERP system (SAP S/4HANA)
- Data encryption at rest and in transit
- 99.9% uptime SLA
- GDPR compliance for our Berlin office

### Integration Requirements
- Bidirectional sync with SAP S/4HANA (orders, inventory, pricing)
- Microsoft 365 (email, calendar, Teams)
- DocuSign for contract e-signatures
- Tableau for advanced analytics (we already have licenses)

## 3. Budget & Timeline

- **Budget:** $400,000 - $500,000 total (including implementation and first-year
  licensing)
- **Timeline:** Go-live by January 2027 (6-month implementation window)
- **Training:** Need onsite training for 180 sales reps + 12 managers

## 4. Evaluation Criteria

- Technical fit and architecture
- Total cost of ownership (3-year view)
- Implementation timeline and methodology
- Vendor experience with manufacturing-sector CRM migrations
- Ongoing support and SLA terms

## 5. Submission Instructions

Please provide:
1. A technical solution addressing all requirements above
2. An itemized pricing proposal with licensing, implementation, and support costs
3. A proposed project timeline

We'd appreciate a **15% multi-year discount** if we commit to a 3-year contract
upfront.
